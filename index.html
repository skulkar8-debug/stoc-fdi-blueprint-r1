Section 02

Our approach

Three numbered steps, consistent with how STOC describes process on other services pages (simple, practical, and execution-focused).

3-step framework
1. Centralize and standardize the data. Aggregate financial, operational, CRM, and site-level inputs into a consistent reporting structure so teams operate from one version of the truth.
2. Build driver-based insight. Develop KPI trees, unit economics, cohort views, and variance bridges that connect operating drivers to revenue, margin, cash flow, and valuation.
3. Enable ongoing decision-making. Embed dashboards, reporting cadences, and scenario models that support deal teams, portfolio leadership, boards, and lenders throughout the hold period.
CTA copy (no button): "Let’s turn fragmented data into investment-grade insight. Reach out to schedule a discovery session with our FDI team." - mirrors CDS "Let’s build your acquisition pipeline..." closing line.
